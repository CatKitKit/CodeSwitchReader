🃏 How Flashcards Work

Example (in reader mode):

▶︎ I am a dog ↖. ➕ ▶︎ She is a cat. ➕

Click on the word to save to notebook. The app grabs the context automatically.

Assuming your target word is 'dog', your notebook will now look like this:

1. dog
2. I am a dog.

Hit 📤 Send to Deck, open 🗂️ Study Cards, and you will see your deck!

Fill-in-the-blank✨
The app automatically turns your pairs into fill-in-the-blank questions

I am a ___.

Ready? Choose a deck language, click 📤 Send to Deck, and then open 🗂️ Study Cards!

💡 Tips

Learning JP, CN, or HK?
Click the ⚙️ gear icon in the top right to enable smart word-splitting and automatic Furigana, Pinyin, or Jyutping!

🎛️ Pro Controls

👁️ Show / 🎧 Audio (Dual Track)
Uncheck 👁️ Show to hide text (for listening practice).
Uncheck 🎧 Audio to mute a language (like a subtitle track).
⏱️ Shadowing Timer
Adds a silence gap (0s - 6s) after every sentence. Great for repeating what you just heard aloud!
Speed Sliders
Each voice has its own independent speed. Set your English fast (2.0x) and your Japanese slow (0.8x)!

🪄 Auto-Align Ebooks (txt and epub)

Auto-Aligner takes the same ebook of 2 different languages, matching them up sentence-by-sentence the best it can, making it into an easy-to-read bilingual version. Simply hit 'Play' as soon as they are imported into the reader! Don't worry about any settings!

* Text might misalign due to structural differences of the languages.
